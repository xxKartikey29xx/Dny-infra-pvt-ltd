Who We Are

DNYANESHWAR INFRA PVT.LTD. is a renowned contracting firm specializing in structural repairs, waterproofing, and painting services. Established 25 years ago, we have built a strong tradition of innovation, consistently striving to elevate our techniques in structural repairs, waterproofing, and painting services. Our unwavering commitment to quality and continuous improvement has earned us a reputation as a trusted in the field . We have a team of professional engineers with extensive experience in material science and other Application techniques. which appreciates important aspects of the business world such as economics, Communication and the need to focus on a particular problem without pursuing costly and unnecessary diversions. The strong practical background of the company and support from architects and consultants structure engineer enable. Effective and economical solutions to any civil engineering problems using state-of-the-art techniques.

Our Mission

We believe in providing our clients, practical engineering solutions to meet the particular structure's needs. We are especially mindful of concerns for cost, as well as the need to maintain a high degree of structural integrity. Our team approach allows us to work closely with clients, look out for their interest and genuinely care about each project's outcome.
